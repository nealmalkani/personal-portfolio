Help Support Carderock's Computer Lab and Instruction
In order to maintain the level of computer instruction our children receive, the CSES Educational Foundation annually turns to parents for financial support. Carderock is unique in the quality of its computer lab and instruction, but we need your donation to maintain that level. Due to the ongoing support of our parent community, every child at Carderock receives specialized computer instruction in a fully equipped computer lab.
