Meet the Committee


Commonwealth Relations

Commonwealth Relations is here to help spread Fresh START Day of Service among all of the campuses. I am eager to share any and all of our resources: from manuals and starter-kits to general advice. I hope to guide the commonwealth locations towards implementation of their own Fresh START programs.


Wendy Fan

Hello! My name is Wendy Fan, currently a junior from West Chester, PA, majoring in biological engineering with a focus of food and bioprocessing in the Schreyer Honors College. I am the 2013 Director of Commonwealth Relations of Fresh START Day of Service. I started my first two years at Penn State Brandywine campus in Media, and one of the main goals I will be trying to achieve this year is to advertise Fresh START at all other branch campuses as much as possible to encourage them to be involved in community service and volunteering projects as well as advertising Fresh START to students who plan to come up to University Park later in the years.
