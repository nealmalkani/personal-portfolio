Meet the Committee


Executive Advisor


Jordan Jozwik

My name is Jordan Jozwik and I serve as the advisor for the FreshSTART planning committee as a part of my role in Student Activities. I am a first-year graduate student in the College Student Affairs program and have a bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering from Tennessee Tech University. At Penn State, I am also involved with the Engineering Ambassadors program.

I believe service learning is an integral part of the college experience because it allows students to become engaged with their community, build a connection to their university, and create relationships that will last beyond their college years. I hope you'll choose to become involved with service (and especially FreshSTART!) while at Penn State. If you would like to get in contact with me, please feel free to email me at jjj188@psu.edu!
