Meet the Committee


Technology

The Director of Technology is responsible for working across committees and ensuring the functionality and success of all things technology related. Along with Public Relations and Outreach, he films, edits, and develops promotional videos and media as well as runs the Facebook, Twitter, and blog accounts. In addition, he develops and maintains the Fresh START website, identifies and obtains technical equipment for events, and fulfills all of the technological needs during the Day of Event.


Neal Malkani
