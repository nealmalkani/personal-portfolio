Meet the Committee


Logistics

The Director of Logistics is responsible for planning the schedules for the day of service, including all special events and involvement fair. Logistics is also responsible for contacting and recruiting speakers, creating detailed timelines, organizing volunteers into their service sites, wtiting travel itineraries, and organizing entertainment.


Brittany Corprew

Hi! I'm Brittany Corprew and I am the Co-Director of Logistics for Fresh START 2013. I am junior double majoring in Psychology and Criminal Justice with a minor in Spanish. I've been involved with Fresh START since my freshman year at University Park. Since my first Fresh START I have done community service all over Centre County, you can say Fresh START really rooted me in service! I think giving back to a community that has welcomed me with open arms is very important. I think the 2013 Executive Committee will work hard to bring you another amazing Fresh START Day of Service. As side from Fresh START, I am involved in the Association of Resident Hall Students (ARHS), Eastview-Nittany Residence Association (ENRA), THON, and the Iota Sweetheart Auxiliary Organization.
