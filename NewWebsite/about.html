Who am I?
Hi! I'm Divya. Yes, like the character in Royal Pains. But no, I'm not in the medical field. I recently graduated from the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University with a degree in Advertising and an emphasis in Advertising Design.

I enjoy traveling. Having grown up in different parts of the world is a gift that I appreciate each day. My global exposures come together in my designs, which uniquely intertwine and unite diverse cultures and art forms.

I have a passion for dance. On canvas and on stage, the principles of design form the foundation of my compositions. On this foundation I am able to unleash my creativity and give meaningful expression to my art.

I live to create. I have a deep understanding of and obsession for design, words, and combining the two. I am excited to enter the realm of advertising to address complex marketing problems with innovative, imaginative solutions.

Feel free to look through my work, and contact me with any comments and/or questions!
