About Me

I am Neal Malkani, a Washington DC-based freelance web designer and developer. I have had opportunities to work with a variety of individuals, institutions, corporate businesses and government organizations. Through these opportunities I have not only helped others with their website design and development needs but also helped myself enhance my interpersonal and academic skills. I have been exposed to a multitude of work environments and have learned to appreciate the importance of good work ethics and data integrity and security.

Making the world a better place is what I strive for. I am team-spirited, passionate about my work and a self-starter. Nothing will leave my hands unperfected or unfinished and I guarantee customer satisfaction through all my work. Feel free to view a sampling of my work here.
