Sunita Ramchandani is the founder of The Winning Essay. As the name indicates, The Winning Essay is dedicated to helping undergraduate and graduate school applicants make a strong case for themselves through their essays and gain acceptance to the universities of their choice. The Winning Essay team helps applicants to produce quality essays that highlight their strengths and talents in winning terms.

Sunita’s background includes employment with the World Bank in Washington D.C. for over 25 years. In her capacity of Board Operations Officer at the World Bank, she wrote a variety of papers and summaries for the World Bank’s Board of Directors in areas that included finance, organizational management, international development, and business. Sunita also was responsible for ensuring the overall quality of her unit’s written products before they were sent to the Office of the President and the Executive Directors.

Sunita brings her strong writing and editing experience to The Winning Essay. She has worked with students who have been admitted into many prestigious universities, including Columbia University, New York University, the University of Pennsylvania, Drexel University, Georgetown University, Rutgers University, the University of Cincinnati, and Syracuse University.
